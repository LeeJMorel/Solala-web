Meet
Solala

Solala is a mental health management tool, designed to help develop emotional intelligence skills, track moods and stress levels, and encourage valuable tools to maintain work/life balance and prevent burn out. They are sort of like a personal assistant for your desk. Solala looks like a plant because they are here for your personal growth!

Take a Breather

With calender integration built in, Solala helps you find time to destress, review your schedule, and tackle the day with confidence

Habit Building

Like a personal assistant for your health, Solala helps you with everything from remembering medication schedules to brushing your teeth

Talk it Out

Here to help you work through your daily challenges big or small, Solala is able to respond to your emotional and social needs.

App Features

Key features in the app help you to manage more than your day to day tasks list, they help you prioritize self care, work to find ways to balance your personal goals with your day to day needs, and help you to communicate in work, school, or life.

Calender Integration

Solala works with your already existing calenders, building you a new schedule that includes rest times, walks, and prompting you to remember important tasks.

Growth Mindset

Help name and grow your own little Solala plant! Solala will interact with you, cheer you on, and be your biggest supporter as you tackle a busy day!

Stress Tracking

Solala can help you track stress responses like muscle tightness, fatigue, or headaches to help you find patterns in your schedule or habits that increase stress levels. Over time Solala can use this data to help you reduce daily stress.

Symptom Tracking is proven more accurate than mood tracking

Responsive Reminders

Builds reminders that work for you to promote better habit building. Keep booking meetings over lunch? Solala will ask if you want to mark yourself as busy in the future to make sure you take a needed break.

Help prioritize yourself in your day, alongside your goals
